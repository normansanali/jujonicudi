Kanye West Remembers Michael Jackson in a Special Way [VIDEO]

Photo Credit: RB/Bauer-Griffin/ Getty Images

Just a few days ago on August 29th, the legendary Michael Jackson would have celebrated his 60th birthday.

The musical Kanye West took to his twitter to share this video of him in the studio mixing up some music. Chance the Rapper is seen in the video as well.

You can hear in the video a sample of a Jackson Five song “Never Can Say Goodbye.”

Kanye titles the tweet “We had to chop this up for Michael’s birthday”

What a wonderful way to honor the late Michael for his special day.
