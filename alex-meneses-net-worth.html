Alex Meneses Net Worth

Alex Meneses:

$6 million

Alex Meneses is an American actress. Prior to her Hollywood career, she worked as a model. As an actress, her first notable role was in Showtime’s comedy series Sherman Oaks in the mid-’90s. She next had a recurring role on the CBS show Dr. Quinn, Medicine Woman where she played the part of Teresa Morales. She has also made notable guest appearances on other shows. This includes Friends, Suddenly Susan, CSI: Crime Scene, NCIS, Psych, and The Goldbergs. She also played Stefania, a recurring role in Everybody Loves Raymond. More recently, she played Isabela Santamaria in the NBC sitcom Telenovela.
