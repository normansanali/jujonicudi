Christine Baranski Net Worth and Salary: Christine Baranski is an American stage and screen actress who has a net worth of $14 million. Christine Baranski is probably most famous for her Emmy Award winning performance in the sitcom Cybill as the character know as “Maryanne Thorpe”. Christine also received an Emmy nomination for her role as “Diane Lockhart” on the TV series The Good Wife. Before making her mark in the television and film industry she was an aspiring Broadway actress, where she was the winner of two Tony Awards.

Christine Baranski was born on May 2, 1952 in Buffalo, New York. Her parents, Virginia and Lucien, were editors of a Polish-language newspaper. Her grandparents were actors in the Polish theater. As you might have guessed, Christine is of Polish descent. She was educated at Villa Maria Academy and later she attended Juilliard. In 1980 Baranaki appeared in the Off-Broadway production of Coming Attractions, this was her Off-Broadway debut and was performed at Playwrights Horizons. She went on to performed in many more Off-Broadway productions including the 1982 play Sally and Marsha at the Manhattan Theatre Club. Hide & Seek was her Broadway debut in 1980; she also starred in other Broadway productions including Hurlburly, The House of Blue Leaves and Rumors. She was the winner of the 1984 Tony Award Best Featured Actress in a Play for her outstanding performance in the Broadway play The Real Thing, written by Tom Stoppard. Some of her most current roles include “Kitty Hurley” in the television series The Bounty Hunter, “Amanda” in the 2011 made for television movie Who is Simon Miller? And as an rreoccurring character beginning in 2009 as Dr. Beverly Hofstadter on the TV hit series The Big Bang Theory.

Personal Life: Christine married fellow actor Matthew Cowles in 1983. They had two daughters and resided for many years in New Preston, Connecticut. Christine and Matthew were married until his death in May 2014.
