Dylan Sprouse Nude Photos Leak, Disney Star Confesses Blunder Online

Oopsies. Former Disney star Dylan Sprouse shared a whole lot of himself in two NSFW photos via Twitter over the weekend — and, unlike many stars before him, the Suite Life of Zack and Cody star quickly fessed up to taking the naked selfies himself online. The fracas went down on Sunday, Dec. 15, when the star, 21, flexed his pecs in a pair of (slightly sheer) boxer briefs in one topless photo; in a second, the former kid actor is totally nude and cupping his private parts.

"Whoops, guess I'm not 14 anymore," Sprouse quickly tweeted, red-faced. "I messed up… but I'd be a fool not to own up to it. Got to move past it I suppose," he added. (Hilariously, he also noted in his Twitter bio that he's a "n00d pic dealer.")

Now a New York University student and video game designer, Dylan added on his Tumblr page: "Thanks for making me laugh through all of this . . .Now would probably be a bad time to add a 'face' page."

No word yet on how the photos leaked, or who did the leaking, but Dylan's twin brother (and Suite Life costar) Cole couldn't help but tease. "Cold in the bathroom huh?"
