Who is Patty Gasso husband?

Jim Gasso is the husband of American softball coach for the Oklahoma Sooners Patty Gasso.

Patty has been the head softball coach at the University of Oklahoma since 1995 and she has led the Sooners team to five national championships (2000, 2013, 2016, 2017, and 2021).

In 28 years at the University of Oklahoma, Patty has compiled a record of 1,384–342–2 and a winning percentage of .802.

Patty’s teams have advanced to the Women’s College World Series fourteen times and won the national championship five times in 2000, 2013, 2016, 2017, and 2021.

Patty’s winning percentage of .801 ranks the highest of Division I college softball coaches with at least 1,000 career wins.

Patty Gasso was born Patricia Marie Froehlich in Los Angeles, and she grew up in Torrance, California.

Patty played softball at California State University, Long Beach and also coached softball at Long Beach City College from 1990 to 1994, compiling a record of 161–59–1.

Who is Patty Gasso husband?

Patty Gasso’s husband Jim is a business owner and coach.

Jim is the owner of Norman’s “IntenCity Sports Training Academy.”
