Gabourey Sidibe Bio, Weight Loss, Married or Single, Husband, Net Worth

Gabourey Sidibe is an American actress best known for her role in Lee Daniels’ 2009 critically inspired film Precious. Her performance in the film, which was her first film role, earned her Best Actress nominations at the Golden Globes and Academy Awards.

She has also starred in the Fox musical drama series Empire, the American Horror Story series, and many other films.

The biography of Gabourey Sidibe

Sidibe was born on May 6, 1983, in Bedford–Stuyvesant, Brooklyn, New York, to a Senegalese father, Ibnou Sidibe, and an African-American mother, Alice Tan Ridley. Her father was a taxi driver and her mother, a special education teacher, gave up her career to become a street performer/singer. She appeared on the fifth season of America’s Got Talent. Gabourey has two brothers and a set of twin sisters. Although she was born in Brooklyn, Gabby, as she was known, grew up in Harlem. Her parents separated when she was 12, sometimes pushing her to live with her aunt while her mother struggled financially.

She attended local colleges such as the borough Manhattan Community College where she has an associate degree. During this time, she held a part-time job as a receptionist and also worked in the telephone sex trade to help pay her school fees. She then went on to study psychology at Mercy College after attending City College of New York. It was during this period that a friend told her that a film audition was being held to find the lead role of Precious.

Gabby was already familiar with the role, having repeatedly read the novel on which the film was based. She was, however, reluctant to attend the auditions as it meant she would have to miss college. She was also preparing for an exam.

To add to that, Gabby had no previous acting experience to use for the audition. Her only acting experience was in school theater. However, she sought inspiration from her own life experiences and knowledge gained during her training in psychology to portray her character in an impactful way.

In an interview, she said her childhood gave her “a lot of practice in acting”, as she grew up pretending to be okay when she wasn’t.

Gabby beat out the more than 300 girls who came to the auditions to land the role. After her performance and the success of the film, everything started to fall into place with offers from all angles. His next role was as a bully in the movie Yelling to the Sky. Other movies she has acted in are Tower Heist, Seven Psychopaths, A White Bird in a Blizzard, etc.

Net value

Gabourey Sidibe has an estimated net worth of $6 million. His earnings come from his various film and serial roles, including episodes of the television series Saturday Night Live, Brooklyn Nine-Nine, and Drunken History among many others. She also pocketed a huge amount of money from her book auction in 2017. She started writing her memoir in 2015 and was able to raise $2,500,000 for it.

Is Gabourey Sidibe Married or Single? Husband

Sidibe has never been married and is currently single. To our knowledge, she is not dating anyone and has never had a relationship with anyone in the entertainment industry or outside. However, she openly talked about what her ideal man would look like.

“I’m 5’4, so if you’re 5’6, I’ll consider you tall because you’re taller than me,” Sidibe said in an interview with People. “If you’re 5’7/8, please also apply: I’ll ride you.”

Weight loss and measurements

Gabourey Sidibe is a perfectly confident young woman with a lot of self-esteem. This is despite the fact that she is an overweight black woman who works in a racist and image-conscious entertainment industry. She managed to be a great role model for plus size women by feeling good about herself and sharing her experiences growing up with a family that tormented her for her weight.

Her life changed, however, when, in 2016, she discovered that she and her brother had diabetes. Following the diagnosis, she made a decision that would change her life. She underwent laparoscopic bariatric surgery in an effort to manage her weight.

From time to time, Gabby posts photos on Instagram of herself, showing the progress she has made. She currently weighs 122 kg and is 5 feet 4 inches tall. His body measures 44-46-46 inches. Her bra size is 38 inches.
