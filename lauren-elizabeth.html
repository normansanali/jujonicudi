Lauren Elizabeth – Professional Life

Professionally, Lauren Elizabeth is a web personality based in the United States who gained massive popularity through her YouTube. The active and beautiful she is one of YouTube’s most popular stars to have crossed a million subscriber benchmark. She handles her channel as a platform to contribute fashion tips, upload vlogs, and talk on serious issues to help encourage other women around the globe.

After finishing her high school studies, she launched her YouTube channel on February 3, 2011. Initially,  she named the channel ‘LoveLaurenElizabeth’, which was later renamed ‘Lauren Elizabeth’. She began uploading videos on YouTube content related to fashion and style tips and started uploading vlogs. Thus she uploaded a video titled “Beginning of Spring HAUL,” on March 7, 2011.

In her early YouTube career, she had no clue that her channel would gather so much attention. Gradually, it started gaining popularity, and started working on different content and uploaded videos of pranks, tags, Q & A’s, shopping hauls, and challenges.

She has immense potential and the ability to continue her revolution in the world of social media and entertainment along with her gorgeous looks. So far, her YouTube channel has currently amassed more than 1.22M subscribers, with almost 102 million views.
