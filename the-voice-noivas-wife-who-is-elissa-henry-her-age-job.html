Who Is The Voice NOIVAS Wife, Elissa Henry? Her Age, Job

When the finale of The Voice Season 3 kicked off on 22 May 2023, each of the Top 5 contestants was tasked with singing one ballad and one upbeat song. NOIVAS from Blake Shelton’s team was one of them. The country singer went with a song of his genre for Chris Stapleton’s “Cold” and for his last song, he went with Lenny Kravitz’s “Fly Away”.

In 2023, other than making a comeback in music with a feature on NBC’s The Voice has continued to have a joyful life with his partner Elissa Henry. Who she is, let us tell you in the rest of the writing.

Meet Elissa Henry, The Voice NOIVAS Wife

NOIVAS has been together with Elissa Henry since 2015.

On 15 February 2022, NOIVAS took to his Instagram @noivaswright to tell the world that he and Elissa just had their 7th Valentine’s Day together. Also, he gushed that it is their first as human parents. To his Queen, he then said “I Love You” and added that he cannot wait to continue to be the perfect example of a loving, protective, and supportive partner for their little one(s) to grow up to be like. This is certainly not the first time, we have seen NOIVAS talk in length about Elissa. There have been many other occasions. Before this, one time, he even confessed that he would not be where he is today if it were not for his “beautiful Black woman, partner and best friend, in my life”.

Until May of 2023, NOIVAS was not once seen referring to Wlissa as his wife though. Even on his BIO on his own website (www.noivas.live/biography), she is only associated with him as his partner.

Elissa Henry And NOIVAS Kids

Together, Elissa Henry and NOIVAS are parents of two beautiful daughters, Bella and Ira, and their fur babies Abby and Bear.

Irys Rose, AKA Ira, turned 6 months old on 22 February 2023. And when this happened, NOIVAS took to his social media to explain how he started celebrating half-birthdays after losing his brother.

Isabella, AKA Bella Roux or little cowgirl, turned 18 months old on 19 January 2023.

Elissa and NOIVAS have always been really excited about their parenting journey. Back in September 2022, before Ira came around, the five of them, including their two fur and one human babies had rented out a villa on a private beach in East Florida for a week for their “Babymoon”. They thought of celebrating the occasion as their last vacation before they are parents of two.

Elissa particularly also enjoys birdwatching with her kids and running around with her dogs.

She also is an avid tennis fan. But, she prefers to play it than watch it. Also, when she is done even having fun with kids, legal dramas are what she tunes onto.

Is Elissa Henry On Instagram?

Yes. Elissa Henry can be found on Instagram. As of 23 May 2023, her account here (@ellie_trill) included 78 posts and 694 followers.

Elissa also showed glimpses of her life on ‘Elissa Henry’ Facebook. “There is nothing enlightened about shrinking so that other people won’t feel insecure around you”, she wrote in her BIO here. Also, here, her most recently updated post was her informing people that voting for the finale of The Voice has closed. Also, she assured fans that no matter who wins, each of the top 5 contestants in the finale, including her own man, will have numerous opportunities to pursue a career in music.

Elissa Henry Age

Elissa Henry was born in 1989. So, she reached the age of 33 in 2022.

Elissa Henry Job

In addition to thanking Elissa for being his muse, partner, and best friend, NOIVAS also has always appreciated Elissa for evolving into a perfect attorney. To the unversed, Elissa is a highly-qualified lawyer specializing in car accidents, probate, and family-based immigration.

Per LinkedIn, she has been the owner/managing attorney at Elissa I. Henry Law Firm in Austin, Texas since November 2016.

Elissa’s passion for this specific career started in 2001 after a pivotal personal experience. Her father was a victim of negligent driving and was forced into a hospital bed for months. During the entire time, Elissa visited her father daily after school and accompanied him for his physical therapy sessions. Her interest and involvement in his case eventually grew as she attended every appointment with his attorney.

Crazy-about-hats NOIVAS, meanwhile, being the country boy that he is, NOIVAS has always settled himself not far than 25 miles outside of the Austin, Texas city limits.

  • Where Did Elissa Henry Earn Her Education?

Elissa Henry studied Doctor of Law (J.D.), Business Litigation at Baylor University School of Law (2013 – 2015). While there, she not only dedicated her time and resources to serving the community but also acted as an associate editor for the Baylor Law Review.

Before that, between 2007 and 2010, she did her B.A. in English and American Literature at New York University. Here, she also was an orientation leader, peer mentor, and academic tutor.

Elissa’s bilingualism in English and Spanish has additionally allowed her to serve a larger audience and diversify her services.
