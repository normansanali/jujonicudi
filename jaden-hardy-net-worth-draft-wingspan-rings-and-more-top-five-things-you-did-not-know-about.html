Jaden Hardy- Net Worth, Draft, Wingspan, Rings and More- Top Five Things You Did Not Know About

The Dallas Mavericks have a superstar in the form of Luka Doncic and have slowly started to build a strong team around their main man with the objective of winning their first championship since 2011. One such player bought in to bolster the squad is 20-year-old Jaden Hardy. The Michigan native was drafted as the 37th overall pick in the second round of the 2022 draft where Paolo Banchero was selected as the top pick by the Orlando Magic. 

After being initially drafted by the Sacramento Kings, he was traded on draft night to the Dallas Mavericks. Hardy was one of the most sought-after high school talents and a consensus five-star recruit as well. Most analysts believed that he would directly enroll for the draft and not bother going to college since he was so highly regarded. 

Despite having offers from the likes of UCLA and Kentucky, Hardy announced that he would join the NBA G League, forgoing college basketball. During his time in the G League, he averaged 17.7 points, 4.6 rebounds, and 3.2 assists per game, shooting 35.1 percent from the field. After one season in the G league, Hardy put his name in for the NBA draft class of 2022. We take a look at a few burning questions fans have about NBA rookie Jaden Hardy.
