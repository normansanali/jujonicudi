Fans Are Left Confused As Lil Uzi Vert Posts A Throwback Photo Of Meek Mill Kissing Nicki Minajs

Is there drama brewing between Nicki Minaj and Lil Uzi Vert?

That’s what fans seem to think after the rapper took to his Instagram Story on September 4 to post a throwback photo of Minaj with then-boyfriend Meek Mill.

The pic sees Mill kiss his former flame’s feet, with the picture itself dating back to around 2015, leaving a lot of people confused.

Uzi added no text or any sort of explanation for fans to understand why he was posting the picture, to begin with, but many took this as an insult to Minaj considering the pair have been friends for years.

Uzi has been vocal in the past, saying that he loves and respects Minaj, whom he collaborated with on their 2017 song “The Way Life Goes, so what was the purpose of posting a photo of the “Hard White” hitmaker with her ex?

Petty, however, didn’t want to hear any of it and warned Mill to keep his distance and leave his wife alone, prompting security officers to interfere and dissolve the heated confrontation.
