Heather Graham, Boyfriend Tyler Harcott Rock Hot Bodies on Beach: Pics

Sexy couple alert! Californication actress Heather Graham proved that age is nothing but a number when she showed off her incredible bikini body on the beach with her equally toned boyfriend Taylor Harcott on Saturday, Jan. 31. 

The 45-year-old blonde was clad in a stylish straw hat and striped bikini while celebrating her Jan. 29th birthday, and enjoyed some hammock time during her Tulum, Mexico getaway with Harcott. Harcott, whose impressive muscles were on display in blue swim trunks, has hosted The Bachelor Canada since 2012 and also helmed TLC’s Junkyard Wars.

Graham and Harcott first stepped out as a couple for the New York City film premiere of Graham’s Horns, with Daniel Radcliffe, back in October. An eyewitness told us that the couple was particularly affectionate in between red carpet interviews, and Graham lit up anytime she introduced Harcott to her costars. When asked what she “enjoys” doing, the Scrubs guest star exclusively told Us she likes to “hang with my boyfriend.”

The cute pair have reportedly been seeing each other for about a year, and are “really happy together and great,” an insider tells Us.
