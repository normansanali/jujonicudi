Manchester City Vs Inter Milan UCL Final Preview, Lineup, And Kickoff!

It’s another football feast as football lovers all around the await Manchester City Vs Inter Milan UCL Final in Istanbul, Turkey.

It will be a rollercoaster clash at the Ataturk Olympic stadium in Istanbul, Turkey as Manchester City battles it out with Inter Milan in the 2022/23 Champions League final.

Match Preview

Having secure a domestic double this season, Manchester City will be aiming to become the second Premier League team to land a treble of Champions League, Premier League, and the FA Cup after Manchester United.

It will be their second Champions League final in the club’s history after the 2021 final against domestic rivals Chelsea.

While the Premier League champions will be looking for their first Champions League title Inter Milan will be aiming for their fourth Champions League title.

The Nerazzuri last won the Champions League in 2010 under the watch of Jose Mourinho.

Inter Milan and Manchester City have met twice in the Champions League and have shared a win apiece.

Team News

Pep Guardiola is heading into the game with his complete star-studded squad.

Kyle Walker was previously a doubt for the game due to a back injury but had returned to training.

It appears Guardiola has settled with his starting eleven for big games. He started all his 10 players that demolished Real Madrid at Etihad against Manchester United in the FA Cup final at Wembley.

Ortega was the only alteration to the squad and will return to the bench while Ederson will start on goal against Inter Milan

Kevin de Bruyne, Erling Haaland, Jack Grealish, IIkay Gundogan, John Stones will all start the game.

Romelu Lukaku and Edin Dzeko will be available for Inter Milan to lead the attack against Manchester City, including Lautaro Martinez.

Andre Onana will start on goal for the Serie A giants with Bastoni, Darminan, Barella, and Mkhitaryan all available for the clash.

Man City probable lineup:
Ederson; Walker, Dias, Akanji; Stones, Rodri; Silva, Gundogan, De Bruyne, Grealish; Haaland

Inter probable lineup:
Onana; Darmian, Acerbi, Bastoni; Dumfries, Barella, Mkhitaryan, Calhanoglu, Dimarco; Martinez, Dzeko

Kickoff

The match will kick off by 19:000GMT which is 8pm Nigerian time. It will be streamed live here on MySportDab. Stay tuned.
