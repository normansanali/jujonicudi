Peyton List Siblings: Meet Spencer List, Phoenix List

Peyton List’s siblings are Spencer List and Phoenix List. Peyton has a twin brother Spencer List, who is just 15 minutes younger than her.

Spencer List is an American actor best known for his role in the Fox show Fringe. He has also been on Law & Order: Special Victims Unit and Jack Ketchum’s Offspring.

Spencer List was born on April 6, 1998, in Florida, United States. Spencer List is 25 years old as of 2023. Phoenix List was born on September 18, 2004, in New York.

Peyton List made an uncredited appearance on the soap opera As the World Turns in 2002. In 2008, List appeared in the feature film 27 Dresses, starring as the young version of the character Jane Nichols.

Peyton List had a role in the romantic comedy film Something Borrowed in 2011. In 2012, Peyton List appeared in the drama film The Trouble with Cali.

Peyton List was cast in the main role of Olivia Richmond in the first season of the supernatural web series Light as a Feather, released in October 2018.
