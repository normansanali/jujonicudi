Ben Stiller Reveals Female Viagra Commercial on Fallon: Watch!

Ben Stiller has the answer for any woman struggling to feel sexually aroused by their husband after years of marriage — and it’s good. Really good!

The Zoolander 2 star was a guest star on Jimmy Fallon’s Tonight Show on Monday, February 8, and during his interview he revealed he had actually recorded his own Super Bowl commercial, but sadly it had been bumped.

As luck would have it, Fallon happened to have a copy of Stiller’s commercial, for Female Viagra no less.

The Blue Steel expert remade the classic male Viagra ad, almost scene for scene, but written with a twist — this form of Viagra is not for erectile dysfunction (which, as he advises, no women at all suffer from), but for another, serious condition effecting over 98 percent of women over 30: not being turned on by their husband anymore.

As Stiller talks in a soothing voice, he explains this pill can be used for special occasions like anniversaries and birthdays, or just “When you’re husband turns over and says, ‘Shall we have sex?'”
