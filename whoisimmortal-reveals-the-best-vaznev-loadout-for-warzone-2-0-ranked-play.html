WhoIsImmortal Reveals the Best Vaznev Loadout for Warzone 2.0 Ranked Play

Warzone expert WhoIsImmortal has shared his take on the best Vaznev Loadout for Warzone 2.0. Here’s an in-depth look at the fastest TTK SMG for Ranked Play.

Call of Duty Warzone 2.0 is the revamped Warzone experience in Modern Warfare 2 engine. The newly designed battle royale features new guns, field upgrades, equipment, a backpack, and a looting system.

Out of the current weapon pool, one particular SMG has managed to steal the spotlight. The Vaznev 9k is currently the most used SMG in MW2 and Warzone 2.0. It has the most manageable recoil out of every gun in the current weapon pool.

Most creators have shared their take on the new Vaznev. However, Warzone expert WhoIsImmortal’s take on the SMG has hit the sweet spot. Here’s an in-depth look at the Vaznev loadout for Ranked Play with the optimal setup.

WhoIsImmortal’s Best Vaznev Loadout

  • Barrel: KAS-1 381MM
  • Tuning: Aiming Walking Speed (-0.27 lb), Aim Down Sight Speed (-0.15 in)
  • Laser: FSS OLE-V Laser
    • Tuning: Sprint to Fire Speed (-0.24 oz), Aim Down Sight Speed (-31.26 ft)
  • Rear Grip: True-Tac Grip
    • Tuning: Aim Down Sight Speed (-0.55 oz), Sprint to Fire Speed (-0.22 in)
  • Stock: Oztreat Stock
    • Tuning: Aim Down Sight Speed (-1.81 oz), Aiming Walking Speed (-1.39 in)
  • Magazine: 45-Round Mag

WholsImmortal has opted out of a muzzle selection for the optimal loadout. The most integral part of the setup is the KAS-1 381MM barrel, which combines the True-Tac Grip and Oztreat Stock. For the loadout to work, players must tune the weapon to the exact spots that unlock the SMG’s full potential. The current meta of Warzone 2.0 Ranked Play emphasizes weapons with low TTK.
