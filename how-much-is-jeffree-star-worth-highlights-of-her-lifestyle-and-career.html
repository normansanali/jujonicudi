How Much is Jeffree Star Worth? Highlights of Her Lifestyle and Career

Jeffree Star has a net worth of $250 million, making him one of the most prominent figures in the beauty business. Jeffree, an American beauty expert, has established himself as a successful model, fashion designer, singer-songwriter, DJ, actor, YouTuber, and entrepreneur.

Famous for creating the cosmetics conglomerate Jeffree Star Cosmetics, Jeffree’s larger-than-life persona has led to a highly successful cosmetics brand and a top-rated YouTube channel. Born in Los Angeles County, California on November 15, 1985, Jeffree was raised by his mother, Marra Shubyann Lindstrom Steininger, who also worked as a model.

Jeffree Star’s Net Worth and Earnings

Jeffree Star’s net worth is $250 million. In 2018, he made $18 million, ranking sixth among the highest-paid YouTube celebrities. His various ventures contribute significantly to his billionaire status. Notably, his music video for the song “Beauty Killer” garnered 13 million views and earned him $24,294. With a firm that sells $100 million in items annually and a successful cosmetics line, Jeffree’s financial success is evident.

His lavish lifestyle includes a vast collection of expensive handbags, designer clothing, and a luxurious home. Jeffree, alongside his partner Nathan, indulges in extravagant celebrations and gifts. Furthermore, he has exhibited a philanthropic side, partaking in charitable activities and donations.

Charitable Endeavors

Jeffree is actively involved in philanthropy, providing financial assistance to individuals in need. He also participated in a global tree-planting project, contributing to the planting of 20 million trees globally by 2020. Moreover, he offered financial support for burial expenses and charitable contributions toward various causes.

Professional Career

Jeffree’s passion for cosmetics has been ingrained since childhood, leading him to pursue a career as a makeup artist and ultimately establish Jeffree Star Cosmetics. Initially known on Myspace, where he was the site’s most-followed individual, Jeffree transitioned to establishing himself as a mega-influencer and entrepreneur. His music career, which included the release of the album “Beauty Killer,” ultimately paved the way for his focus on the cosmetics profession and the creation of his successful YouTube channel.
