World Settings for Experienced Players

For players with some survival experience, the default settings in Palworld offer a well-balanced gaming experience. However, for those seeking a bit more excitement, there are adjustments available to enhance the thrill of the game.

Conversely, seasoned players in search of a formidable challenge can opt for the hard difficulty setting. Here are the recommended settings for each difficulty level:

  • Day Time Speed, Night Time Speed: 1 for all difficulties
  • EXP Rate, Pal Capture Rate, Pal Appearance Rate: Varies per difficulty
  • Damage Multipliers: Tailor based on preferred challenge level
  • Player and Pal Hunger, Stamina, Auto HP Regeneration Rates: Adjust for balance
  • Gatherable Objects Respawn Interval: Varies per difficulty
  • Dropped Items Multiplier, Death Penalty, Enable Raid Events: Customize based on player preference

These settings serve as a comprehensive guide, catering to players of varying experience levels and preferences.
