The Meaning Behind The Song: Remember December by Wintertime Zi &amp; Lil Yachty

As a fan of Wintertime Zi and Lil Yachty, one of my favorite songs by them is “Remember December.” Whenever I listen to this song, it takes me back to a special event in my life – my birthday, which falls in December. The lyrics and the vibe of the song perfectly capture the feeling of that time, making it even more meaningful to me.

The hook of the song, sung by Wintertime Zi, goes, “I don’t remember, I don’t remember, Bitch I’m so cold, yeah I’m so December, Chasing this cheddar, chasing this cheddar, She gon’ take drugs with me if I let her.” At first glance, these lyrics may seem straightforward, but there’s a deeper meaning to them.

To me, “I don’t remember” symbolizes the idea of letting go of the past and embracing the present. It’s about living in the moment and not dwelling on what has already happened. December, often associated with the end of the year, signifies a fresh start and the opportunity to chase after your goals and dreams. The line about chasing cheddar reflects the pursuit of success and financial stability.

The verses, performed by Wintertime Zi and Lil Yachty, further emphasize these themes. Wintertime Zi raps about his determination to achieve his desires and how he prioritizes his goals over distractions like relationships. He mentions fishing with Lil Boat (Lil Yachty), which can be interpreted as the two artists working together to achieve their dreams. The references to cold as the winter, getting a million, and speaking nothing but facts all speak to their commitment to success and authenticity.

Lil Yachty’s verse adds depth to the song, discussing his rise to fame and the challenges he faces along the way. He addresses the critics and expresses his trust issues with others, highlighting that he relies on his own determination and skill to succeed. He acknowledges his growing fanbase and the attention he receives from others, but ultimately prioritizes his own journey.

Overall, “Remember December” is not just a catchy song but has a deeper message about living in the present, chasing your dreams, and overcoming obstacles. The lyrics reflect the mindset of Wintertime Zi and Lil Yachty as they navigate the music industry and strive for success on their terms.

For me, this song will always hold a special place in my heart because of the memories it evokes. Whenever I listen to it, I am reminded of the joy and excitement I feel during December, especially on my birthday. It serves as a reminder to stay focused, keep chasing my goals, and embrace new beginnings.

In conclusion, “Remember December” by Wintertime Zi & Lil Yachty is more than just a song. Its meaningful lyrics and catchy beat encapsulate the mindset of chasing success and living in the present. Whether you have personal memories associated with it or not, this song serves as a reminder to stay true to yourself and keep working towards your dreams.
