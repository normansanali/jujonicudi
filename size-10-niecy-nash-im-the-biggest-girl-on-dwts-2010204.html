Size 10 Niecy Nash: &quot;I'm the Biggest Girl&quot; on DWTS

Dancing With the Stars' Niecy Nash has embraced her jiggles.

"As a size 10, I'm the biggest girl in this competition," the actress, 40, and mom of three (son Dominic, 18, and daughters Donielle, 15, and Dia, 10, with ex Don Nash) tells UsMagazine.com. "But I don't compare. I stay in my own lane."

"It's a lot of core work," continues Nash, who loves to work out to "Bootylicious" by Destiny's Child. (She and Van Amstel received an 18 out of 30 for their Monday night dance to "La Bamba.")
