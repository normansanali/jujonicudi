Leonardo DiCaprios ex Nina Agdal just got engaged to Logan Paul

YouTuber turned WWE star Logan Paul recently announced that he is engaged to his girlfriend, Nina Agdal. The two publicly announced their relationship back in 2022 after Agdal’s four-year relationship with Jack Brinkley-Cook ended in 2021.

The couple announced the engagement on Instagram, unveiling that the proposal took place in Lake Como, Italy. The post features four photos of the proposal, followed by the caption “Engaged to my best friend.”

Agdal has an extensive dating history. Prior to her relationship with Paul, she dated Leonardo DiCaprio from 2016 to 2017. She also dated Maroon 5’s lead singer, Adam Levine, in 2013. The same can be said for Paul, who had once dated Chloe Bennet in 2018.

Paul was first spotted romantically involved with Agdal in Mykonos in April 2022, when the two were seen kissing and spending time together on the beach. Additionally, there were previous reports of them being seen in restaurants in London and Manhattan.

Before he was known for his WWE career, Paul was known for his online content on YouTube. Unfortunately, some of his videos have been seen as controversial. One notable example is his 2017 vlog series in Japan, where he and his friends reacted inappropriately when they came across a body in a Japanese forest. Since then, the content creator has gone through major changes to fix his image and reputation, which led to his boxing and wrestling career.

Paul and his former YouTube rival, KSI, recently launched their newest energy sports drink, Prime. An ad for the sports drink was spotted during the 2023 Super Bowl, on the same day the two YouTubers embarked on a promotional tour in Australia. According to the official Prime website, it is the official sports drink of the UFC.

Meanwhile, Agdal is known for her successful modeling career. She started her career in 2010 and was featured in Sports Illustrated in 2012. Additionally, she has made two cameo appearances in the films Don Jon and Entourage.

There is much anticipation surrounding the couple’s forthcoming announcement and their future wedding arrangements, although nothing has been confirmed at this time.
