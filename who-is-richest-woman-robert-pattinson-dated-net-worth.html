Who Is The Richest Woman Robert Pattinson Dated, And Is She Richer Than He Is?

Hollywood star Robert Pattinson — who is surely best known for his portrayal of Edward Cullen in The Twilight Saga — has dated numerous famous women over the years. Currently, the actor can be seen as the DC Comics superhero Batman in Matt Reeves' movie The Batman which was released in 2022.

Today, we're taking a look at just how wealthy the women that Pattinson has been linked to are today. From Suki Waterhouse to Kristen Stewart — are any of the women the actor dated richer than he is?

9 Dylan Penn Has A Net Worth Of $4 Million

Kicking the list off is actor Sean Penn's daughter, model Dylan Penn. Robert Pattinson and Dylan Penn dated between September and November 2013. Apart from modeling, Dylan Penn also has experience as an actress as she appeared in the horror movie Condemned. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Dylan Penn is currently estimated to have a net worth of $4 million.

8 FKA Twigs Has A Net Worth Of $4 Million

Next on the list is British singer FKA Twigs who rose to fame in the early 2010s. Robert Pattinson and FKA Twigs started dating in July 2014 and in March 2015 the two got engaged. However, they never made it to the altar and in August 2017, the couple separated. Currently, FKA Twigs — who revealed she's still grateful for her relationship with Pattinson — is estimated to have a net worth of $4 million, meaning that she shares her spot with Dylan Penn.

7 Riley Keough Has A Net Worth: $5 Million

Let's move on to actress Riley Keough who was linked to Robert Pattinson between July and August 2013.

Keough rose to fame in the 2010s, and she starred in projects like The Good Doctor, Magic Mike, and Mad Max: Fury Road. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Riley Keough is currently has a net worth of $5 million.

6 Mia Wasikowska Has A Net Worth Of $8 Million

Mia Wasikowska, who dated Robert Pattinson between September 2017 and July 2018,is next. The actress rose to prominence after playing Alice in Tim Burton's 2010 movie Alice in Wonderland. Apart from this project, she's also known for starring in Jane Eyre, The Kids Are All Right, and Crimson Peak. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Mia Wasikowska is currently estimated to have a net worth of $8 million.

5 Suki Waterhouse Has A Net Worth Of $10 Million

Next up is Robert Pattinson's current partner, Suki Waterhouse. The actor met the model back in 2018, and by August of that year, the two have already started dating. Over the past four years, Pattinson and Waterhouse have been spotted together on numerous occasions. Suki Waterhouse started modeling at the age of 16, and over the course of her career, she worked with brands like Topshop, H&M, Burberry, Tommy Hilfiger, and many more. Apart from modeling, Waterhouse has also explored the world of acting, and she appeared in projects like Love Rosie, The Divergent Series: Insurgent, The Girl Who Invented Kissing, and Billionaire Boys Club. Currently, Suki Waterhouse is estimated to have a net worth of $10 million.

4 Nikki Reed Has A Net Worth Of $12 Million

Let's move on to actress Nikki Reed who starred alongside Robert Pattinson in The Twilight Saga. Between February and June 2009, the two co-stars were linked to each other.

3 Kristen Stewart Has A Net Worth Of $70 Million

Another Twilight co-star whom Robert Pattinson dated is Kristen Stewart who played his love interest in the movies. The two actors started dating in the early summer of 2009 but after four years the couple broke up in May 2013. After The Twilight Saga, Kristen Stewart starred in numerous famous projects such as Snow White and the Huntsman, Charlie's Angels, Happiest Season, and Spencer. As of writing, Kristen Stewart is currently estimated to have a net worth of $70 million.

2 Robert Pattinson Has A Net Worth Of $100 Million

While there is no doubt that all of Robert Pattinson's exes are super-rich, the famous actor still has a higher net worth than almost all of them. Pattinson — who is known for starring in projects like Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire, Water for Elephants, Tenet, and The Batman — is currently estimated to have an impressive net worth of $100 million.

1 Katy Perry Has Net Worth Of $330 Million

Lastly, wrapping the list up as the richest woman Robert Pattinson was linked to is musician Katy Perry. However, a relationship between the two was never confirmed, but they were linked to each other in 2012, which is why we included her on the list. Katy Perry rose to fame in the late 2000s and over the course of her career, she released six successful studio albums.
