When is Memorial 2023?

Memorial Day 2023 falls on Monday, May 29. Memorial Day is an American federal holiday that always falls on the last Monday in May to honor the men and women who died while serving in the U.S. military. 

When do Memorial Day Apple sales start?

Generally speaking, Memorial Day sales on tech products tend to be concentrated around Memorial Day itself, the preceding weekend, and in some cases the preceding week too. We usually see the big hardware stores like Lowes and Home Depot begin their Memorial Day sales in early May, but the tech retailers like Amazon and Best Buy usually kick off their sales later in the month.

For Memorial Day Apple deals specifically, based on previous events we'd say it's a great idea to keep an eye out from Monday, May 22nd onwards but don't be in a rush to pick anything up. Retailers are likely to reserve their best deals for Monday, May 29, so if you want to snag the lowest price possible, you should wait for Memorial Day proper.
