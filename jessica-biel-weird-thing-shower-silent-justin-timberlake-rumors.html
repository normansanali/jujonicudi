Jessica Biel Reveals Weird Thing She Does In Shower As She Stays Silent On Justin Timberlake Rumors

Highlights

  • Jessica Biel admits to enjoying the simple pleasure of eating in the shower, using a built-in shelf to hold her food while she washes up.
  • She started this habit as a way to have some "me time" and escape from her two sons.
  • Speculation continues about the state of Jessica and Justin's marriage, with reports suggesting trust issues and past infidelity have resurfaced.

There continues to be a ton of speculation surrounding Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel’s marriage, as some reports suggest she’s struggling to get over the backlash Justin faced with the release of Britney Spears’ memoir The Woman in Me, which exposes the alleged mistreatment he put the pop star through during their relationship.

While Biel has declined to comment on the speculation surrounding her relationship, the actress did make a surprising confession recently about the weird habit she has when showering, which she wants to help normalize.

Jessica Biel Admits She Eats In The Shower

Posting to TikTok, Jessica admitted she believes one of life’s simplest pleasures is simply eating while in the shower. “It’s pretty simple, guys,” she revealed. “You can do this. I find it deeply satisfying.”

The 7th Heaven alum went on to explain her process, saying she’ll use a built-in shelf to rest the food on when she needs her hands for other things. Jessica said she often take yogurt or a coffee in while she’s scrubbing up.

“I like to take a bite or a sip and put it on the ledge, and then you do your thing,” she explained. “You wash your hair — keep the soap out — that’s a big deal.”

“The only tricky thing is when you’re chewing, you got to keep your mouth closed because I still like to get under the water while I’m chewing, and for whatever reason, I want to open my mouth at the same time and spit water,” Jessica went on.

She also revealed exactly why she started eating in the shower, admitting it has to do with wanting to escape her two sons and have some me time.

“Chew, do not open the mouth [and] do not let the shower water in. There you go, enjoy your shower-consuming,” she advised her fans, adding eating in the show is “a relief in so many ways.”

Jessica And Justin Are Allegedly Struggling With Trust Issues

Despite Jessica’s candid confession about her unconventional showering habit, the actress didn’t reference Timberlake. The couple began dating in 2007 before tying the knot on October 19, 2012, in Italy. The couple welcomed their first child, a son named Silas, in April 2015. Their second child, name Phinneas, was born in 2020.

They may have renewed their vows in 2022, but there are an increasing number of reports that Biel and Timberlake’s marriage is on the rocks.

“It’s a nightmare that just won’t go away,” a source told In Touch last fall . “It’s been very hard on their marriage.”

The source said Britney’s allegations have also brought up the couple’s past trust issues, including when Justin was accused of cheating with actress Olivia Munn in 2010. He was later caught holding hands with his Palmer co-star Alisha Wainwright during an evening out; though Timberlake apologized, he denied anything inappropriate happened.

“Jessica has buried her head in the sand for so long when it comes to Justin’s sketchy past,” the source continued. “She refused to believe he’d cheated on her with Olivia, but that thing with Alisha was a tough pill to swallow because there were the pictures. If she hadn’t seen it with her own eyes, maybe she wouldn’t have believed it, but it was staring her in the face.”

For now, Justin and Jessica appear to be putting on a loyal front.
