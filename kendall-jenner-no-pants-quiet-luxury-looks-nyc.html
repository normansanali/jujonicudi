See Kendall Jenner Step Out in No-Pants and Quiet-Luxury Looks in NYC

Kendall Jenner has applied her supermodel spin to two beloved trends.

Earlier this week, the model landed back in NYC after her whirlwind trip to Paris for Fashion Week. Though it's only been a few days since she arrived back in the States, the star has already been spotted in two casual-chic looks while out and about in the city.

On Thursday night, the 818 founder stepped out her latest no-pants look, layering a sleek, slightly-oversized navy blazer layered over white button-down blouse worn as a dress. She added plain black loafers from The Row (worn without socks), dainty silver earrings, black shades, and a slouchy black leather tote, topping off the ensemble with a faded blue Yankees cap.

Gotham//Getty Images

For her Friday afternoon trip to the The Brant Foundation Art Study Center in NYC's Tribeca neighborhood, the star continued her quiet luxury streak while carrying the trend's ultimate bag. She paired a navy short-sleeved crop top with dark wide-leg jeans, adding the same black loafers and black shades. Further accessories included another Yankees cap, this one camouflage-print with navy lettering; a gold watch; and an oxblood Birkin, which she styled with a coordinating red leather belt.

Gotham//Getty Images

Throughout Jenner's busy week following the Jacquemus menswear show, the model wore a series of sexy and sophisticated dresses. For one look, she paid homage to Carrie Bradshaw in a gray bodycon minidress by St. Agni, styled with strappy black sandals with golden embellishments and a woven black leather tote by Bottega Veneta. On another busy day, the star stepped out in contrasting day-and-night styles: a glamorous slate-gray dress with a sleek chest cutout and a pastel-yellow halter gown entirely covered in sequins.
