CM Punk & Marty Scurll Announced For C2E2

Former WWE Superstar CM Punk will join Ring of Honor‘s Marty Scurll for a meet and greet at the upcoming C2E2 event. The duo will be signing autographs and posing for pictures with fans courtesy of Pro Wrestling Tees:

Come visit PWTees at @c2e2. Autographs, Photo Ops, Posters, Hats & EXCLUSIVE shirts by @cmpunk & @martyscurll! Plus tons more exclusives from @MattJackson13 @nickjacksonyb @KennyOmegamanX & @allelitewrestling who will also be joining us! pic.twitter.com/nCim4DJ18F

— One Hour Tees (@OneHourTees) February 22, 2019

CM Punk commented on the announcement and even promised a thumb wrestling match with Scurll:

We are thumb wrestling at @c2e2

— Coach (@CMPunk) February 23, 2019

C2E2 takes place March 22nd-23rd at Chicago’s McCormick Center. CM Punk and Mary Scurll join previously announced guests The Young Bucks, Kenny Omega, and Jim Cornette. Readers may visit C2E2 for ticketing information.
