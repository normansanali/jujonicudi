Jasika Nicole

Jasika Nicole is an American actor and cartoonist best known for her roles as Agent Astrid Farnsworth on the hit Fox series Fringe and Dr. Carly Lever on the medical drama series The Good Doctor. Claire J. Savage, a photographer, is her openly lesbian boyfriend.

Birmingham, Alabama is where She was born

Jasika Nicole was born on April 10, 1980, under the sign of Aries in Birmingham, Alabama, United States of America. She is 40 years old right now. Jasika has no brothers or sisters; she is the lone child of her parents. Nicole is of white ancestry and of American nationality. She is 5 feet 2 inches (157 cm) tall and weighs 55 kilograms. Nicole received her schooling at Catawba College in Salisbury, North Carolina, where she studied theatre, dance, voice, and studio art.

Career highlights include appearances on Fringe and The Good Doctor

Jasika Nicole made her acting debut as Gisella in the NBC criminal drama series Law & Order: Criminal Intent’s episode “Scared Crazy.” Later that year, she portrayed Dora in two episodes of The Return of Jezebel James, “Sarah Takes a Bullet” and “Paragraph Two, Section Three.” Nicole starred as Astrid Farnsworth in the Fox popular series Fringe from 2008 to 2013, with Anna Torv, Joshua Jackson, and Lance Reddick.

Since then, she has been in dozens of television episodes, including Scandal, Major Crimes, Hell’s Kitchen, Adventure Time, Underground, Justice League Action, Danger & Eggs, and Station 19.

Nicole was cast as series regular Dr. Carly Lever in the medical drama series The Good Doctor in 2017. Her film credits include Misdirection, A Christmas in New York, Suicide Kale, and She’s Out of My League.
