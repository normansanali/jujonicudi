Katy (Japanese: カエデ Kaede) is a Supporter card. It is part of the Scarlet & Violet expansion.

Card text

Shuffle your hand into your deck. Then, draw 8 cards. Your turn ends.
You may play only 1 Supporter card during your turn (before your attack).

Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Full Art ultra rare card in the Scarlet & Violet expansion, first released in the Japanese Violet ex expansion. Both prints feature artwork by Akira Komayama.

Gallery

Trivia

Origin

Katy is the Gym Leader of the Cortondo Gym in Paldea. The Cortondo Gym's battle court is featured in the Regular print's illustration.
