    Jenn Harcher Meteorologist FOX 8

    Jenn is an American meteorologist who works with Fox 8 Weather team in Cleveland, Ohio, USA. Previously, before joining Fox 8 News, she worked with WABI TV5 News in Bangor, Maine where she covered severe wind chills, nor’easters, and ice storms.

    From there she headed south and worked at WITN in Greenville, North Carolina covering a record-breaking summer for severe storms, dangerous heat waves, and tropical storms. For the past 10 years, she has forecast Cleveland’s ever-changing weather.

    When she is not chasing storms or tracking tornadoes, you can find Jenn and her husband Jeff chasing after their two little boys. A 6 and 8-year-old keep her very busy! In her spare time, Jenn enjoys teeing it up, kayaking, fishing, and hiking our many amazing parks in Northeast Ohio. She is a huge Buckeye, Browns, and Indians fan, naturally, but loves anything Cleveland!.
