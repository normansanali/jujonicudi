Alberto Roemmerss Net Worth, Biography, Career, Awards, Fact and Life Story

Alberto Roemmers Net Worth: Alberto Roemmers is a German-native businessman and current resident of Argentina who has a net worth of $2.3 billion. Alberto Roemmers is the head of Laboratorios Roemmers, the largest pharmaceutical group in Argentina. The pharmaceutical giant, which is the main source of Roemmers’ fortune, was founded by his father back in 1921. By 1961, the company witnessed a major international expansion as it has already set a firm network of distribution that spanned across 17 countries in Latin America. Today, Laboratorios Roemmers is considered as a leader in terms of unit sales and turnover in the pharmaceutical market in Latin America. Aside from generating big profits, the company has also donated a considerable amount of products to hospitals and state health. Plus, it has awarded grants to over 1,000 research projects to date. In addition to Laboratorios Roemmers, Alberto invested in a real estate project in Uruguay and he produces olive oil in three different provinces in Argentina, as well as he is a shareholder in another pharmaceutical company called Gador. A man who appreciates art, especially the work of Fernando Fader, he owns a personal collection of paintings that mostly includes pieces from the French- born Argentine impressionist painter.
