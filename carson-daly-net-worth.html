What is Carson Daly’s Net Worth and Salary?

Carson Daly, a California native, has a net worth of $40 million. He rose to fame as a VJ for MTV, but later hosted multiple reality shows and worked as a radio DJ. Today, Daly is best known as a host for various NBC programs.

Early Life and Career of Carson Daly

Carson Jones Daly was born in Santa Monica, California in 1973. His mother was a TV personality, and his father passed away shortly after his birth. Daly attended high school where he was part of the golf team. He graduated in 1991, but instead of attending college, he pursued a career as a pro golfer. Daly also worked as a radio DJ at the College of the Desert where he earned the nickname “Kid Carson.” He even interned for Jimmy Kimmel when Kimmel hosted a radio show in Palm Springs. The two had known each other since Kimmel was a child living in Las Vegas.

Carson Daly’s Career Highlights

Carson Daly started his career in radio before being recruited by MTV to serve as a VJ. He hosted MTV Live and DRL, where he interviewed celebrity guests and introduced music videos. Daly then became the host of NBC’s Last Call With Carson Daly, as well as the network’s New Year’s Eve special. He also hosted and served as an executive producer for NBC’s reality competition show, The Voice. In addition, Daly became the social media correspondent for the Today show and has had small acting roles on My Name is Earl and Chappelle’s Show.

Carson Daly’s Personal Life

Carson Daly dated Tara Reid in 2000 and got engaged but called it off in 2001. In 2015, he married Siri Pinter, a food blogger who is the daughter of actor Marc Pinter. The couple has four children together. Daly identifies as a Catholic and has opened up about his struggles with anxiety.

Carson Daly’s Salary

Carson Daly earns a salary of $5 million per year for hosting The Voice. He also earns $5 million per year for his work on The Today Show. Therefore, his total annual salary from NBC is $10 million.
