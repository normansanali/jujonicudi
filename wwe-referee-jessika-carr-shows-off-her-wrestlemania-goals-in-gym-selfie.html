WWE Referee Jessika Carr Shows Off Her WrestleMania Goals In Gym Selfie

Jessika Carr is not the one to be messed with and she’s making sure that you know it. The SmackDown referee took to Twitter Tuesday (March 22) to show off her tremendous gains ahead of WrestleMania 38.

Jessika said she’s close to the fitness goal she has in mind for Mania 38. From the looks of her latest picture, it seems that Jessica is more than ready to maker her presence known on the grandest stage of them all.

Very close to my ‘Mania goal 💪🏻💪🏻💪🏻 Less than 2 weeks away!!!! #WrestleMania

Jessika signed with WWE in 2017, becoming the company’s first female referee since the eighties. Prior to signing with WWE, Jessika worked the independent circuit under the ring names Jessie Kaye and Kennadi Brink.

She also has the distinction of being the first female referee in Saudi Arabia when she visited the country as part of WWE’s Crown Jewel premium live event, which took place on October 20, 2021. We’ll have to see what’s next for her, but Jessika Carr will continue living her dream.
