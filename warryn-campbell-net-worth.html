Warren Campbell Net Worth

Warren Campbell is an American record producer specializing in gospel music and R&B. He has a net worth of $15 million dollars. Campbell began his music career in his late teens, performing with the group New Vision and playing several instruments. He later focused on music production and got his start as a session musician and producer under the guidance of DJ Quik.

Campbell has produced hit songs for many artists, including Kanye West, Jennifer Hudson, and Alicia Keys. He also discovered the gospel duo, Mary Mary, and while producing many of their songs, he fell in love with and married Mary Mary member, Erica Atkins. In addition to his work as a music producer, Campbell is a member of the gospel group The Soul Seekers and was formerly Vice-President of A&R at Elektra Records. He has his own label called, My Block.

Campbell was diagnosed with kidney cancer in 2008 and had an emergency operation to remove one kidney. He is currently appearing on the WEtv reality series that chronicles the musical life of his wife and her sister, Tina Atkins Campbell, called “Mary Mary”. The couple has three children, two girls (Krista and Zaya) and one boy (Warren Campbell III).
