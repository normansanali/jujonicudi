Selena Gomez, Zedd Double Date With Zac Efron and Girlfriend Sami Miro

Oh my lanta! Selena Gomez and rumored new beau DJ Zedd were spotted grabbing a bite to eat in Atlanta, Ga., on Friday, Jan. 23 — but they weren’t alone. An insider tells Us Weekly that Justin Bieber’s on-and-off love went on a double date with Zac Efron and his girlfriend Sami Miro.

The foursome dined at STK, and were also joined by some of Gomez’s backup dancers and friends, the source says. Gomez, 22, and fellow Disney alum Efron, 27, feasted on shellfish platters, scallops, and fish. (Efron and model Miro have been dating since October.)
