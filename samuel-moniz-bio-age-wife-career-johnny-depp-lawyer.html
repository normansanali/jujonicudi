Samuel Moniz Career

As of 2022, Samuel Moniz continued to be an associate in the Firm’s Litigation & Arbitration Practice Group. And so far, his practice concentrated on complex arbitration and litigation in state and federal courts.

Samuel’s career has seen him represent a range of clients, from large companies to high-profile individuals, in a wide variety of matters, including contract disputes, fraud, business torts, malpractice, unfair competition, and defamation. He has experience in pre-trial, trial, and appellate work and he is active in pro bono matters.

Where Did Samuel Moniz Recieve His Education?

Samuel Moniz went to UCLA School of Law in Los Angeles, California for his degree in Law. And then, he was admitted to the State Bar of California in December 2016. His license has been active ever since.

Over the course, he has dealt with cases like Own Capital, LLC, v. Hyundai Of North Jacksonville, Richard Elparin v. Alex M. Azar II, and Umapathi K. Reddy v. Adarsh Developers, among others.
