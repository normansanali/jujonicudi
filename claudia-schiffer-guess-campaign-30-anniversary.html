[PICS] Guess 30th Anniversary Campaign Stars Veteran Model Claudia Schiffer Hollywood Life

The denim brand is known for their iconic campaigns throughout the years, so what better way to celebrate a major milestone than by enlisting one of the most memorable Guess girls of the past?

From Adriana Lima to Laetitia Casta and Alessandra Ambrosio, the privilege of fronting a Guess campaign has only been awarded to a select few — and these girls make up an elite collection of Guess girls. While there have been many gorgeous gals who have posed for the brand throughout the years, no one could compete with iconic Guess girl veteran Claudia Schiffer. With six campaigns, (from 1989-1991), under her belt she was the perfect choice for the brand’s 30th anniversary campaign — and she looks better than ever, 23 years later!
