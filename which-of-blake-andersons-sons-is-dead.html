Which of Blake Anderson's sons is dead and cause of death?

Utah State head football coach Blake Anderson has lost one of his two sons. His son sadly passed away on Tuesday, March 1, 2022.

Robert Blake Anderson is an American football coach and former player who is currently the head coach at Utah State University.

Table of Content

Which of Blake Anderson’s sons is dead and cause of death?

The death of Anderson’s son was announced by Aggies Athletic Director John Hartwell in a social media post.

“On behalf of Utah State University, the athletics department and the football program,” Hartwell noted, “our thoughts and prayers are with coach Blake Anderson and his family following the tragic death of his son.”

In his social media post, Hartwell did not say which of Anderson’s two sons had died. Neither did he give information about the cause of Anderson’s son’s death.

Before the obituary announcement, Anderson took to Twitter on Monday to share a tweet that may have had something to do with the death of his son.

“God is STILL God in the midst of our broken [heart],” Anderson tweeted.

Who is Blake Anderson’s late wife?

Blake Anderson has been married twice, but all his children come from his first wife Wendy Anderson. The couple married for more than a decade or so, but Wendy sadly passed away in 2019 following a three-year battle with cancer.

Anderson went on to marry his second wife, Brittany Anderson in 2021. They are currently married.

Who are Blake Anderson’s children?

Anderson and his late wife, Wendy, had two sons, Coleton and Cason, three daughters, Callie, Collins, and Ellison.

Since one of his children is gone, he is left with four children now.
