Who Is Cheer's Jada Wooten? Her Bio, Age, Boyfriend, Height, Family

Cheer returned on Netflix with a second season on 12 January 2022 with some Season 1 favorites and with some newest competitors ready to steal the Daytona crown. Jada Wooten is among the newcomers and from Navarro’s rivals Trinity Valley Community College.

And now, we shall tell you all about her in this ‘Jada Wooten Bio’.

Jada Wooten On Netflix’s Cheer Season 2

Cheer Season 2 sees several new cast members Navarro’s latest recruit — Maddy Brum,  the child star Cassadee Dunlap, and Rookie Gillion Rupert — and a few of the team coaches in Navarro’s rivals Trinity Valley Community College that include Khris Franklin, Jeron Hazelwood, tumblers DeVonte Dee Joseph, Angel Rice, and leader Jada Wooten (of course).

So, this season Cheer introduces us to Navarro’s rivals up the road and The Cardinals’ de facto student leader is Jada Wooten. She is a second-year tumbler and flyer.

Just as netflix.com puts throughout the season, she successfully wrangles The Weenies, aka Jaymo, Benji, and Dee, and leads inspirational team chants both in practice and at Daytona.

Before becoming a part of Cheer Season 2, Jada has cheered for Cheer Athletics.

She studied at Trinity Valley Community College from August 2018 until sometime in 2020 and before that graduated from Allatoona High School in 2018.

Further on her BIO, Jada proudly presents herself as a 2021 NCA Collegiate National Champion, 2015 Grand World Champion, a Sam Houston State Cheer, and a three times Bronze Worlds Medalist.

Now, Jada is yet to tell us what her plans are for when her time with the Sam Houston State University (SHSU) co-ed squad comes to an end and more.

Jada Wooten Age

Because Jada Wooten was born on 16 March 2001, she turned 20 years old in 2021.

On the day, Jada turned that age, her family and friends made sure she had the best celebration and among the wishes coming in on social media were her mom’s that said: “Help me wish a Very Happy Birthday to my youngest Baby Girl Jada Wooten! The BIG 20! Happy Birthday Baby! I Love You”.

Does Jada Wooten Have A Boyfriend?

Jada Wooten’s relationship status on Facebook is marked as ‘single’.

On 17 December 2019, Jada’s dad took a screenshot of Jada and her then-boyfriend and proudly welcomed him into the family writing “My youngest Baby and her boyfriend!!! SPEECHLESS” as the caption.

But because the very post no longer existed, maybe Jada is no longer seeing her that boyfriend.

Be that as it may, Jada seemed content when in a relationship with him. “best friend, travel partner, supporter & more…I love & appreciate you” Jada had expressed next to the picture of them that her dad took a screenshot of.

Also so you know, back in 2014, in a retweet, Jada spoke out her thought on relationships saying “if u think it’s “too gay” to do nice things for a girl, you’re probably “too immature” for a relationship anyway”.

Jada Wooten Height

Thin yet toned Jada Wooten in her attractive physique stood below 5′ 7” tall at the age of 20.

Jada Wooten Family

Jada Wooten’s mom Tess Wooten is on Twitter @wootsworld. Also, from what she writes on her Facebook, she is from York, Pennsylvania, and is currently a Kennesaw, Georgia resident.

While Jada’s dad Tawn Wooten is a realtor/real estate consultant at HomeSmart, eXp Realty, and Your Home Sold Guaranteed Or I’ll Buy It – The Miskin Team and regional sales manager at LeafFilter. Also, so you know, he studied at Pennsylvania State University and went to Mckeesport Area Senior High School.

Jada, it seems, also has two sisters Kala Wooten and Lauren Robbins. Kala, here, studied Accounting at Grove City College and Lauren studied Software Engineering at Kennesaw State University before she started working at Sparkles Family Fun Centers of Kennesaw and Kupulau Child Development Center. Also, Kala turned 24 in September 2021, and by then she already had a new apartment in the city.

So, Jada’s mom and dad, her and her elder siblings seemingly make a very happy family.

Jada’s family is also close to her uncle Tyler Wooten of Atlanta, Georgia. From what’s been stated on his Facebook BIO, he went to Mckeesport Area Senior and State College Area high schools.
