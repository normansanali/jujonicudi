Anne Carter Robertson: Meet Pat Robertson daughter

Pat Robertson is a well-known and renowned American media mogul, televangelist, and political commentator. He is also a former Republican presidential candidate, and former Southern Baptist minister.

Pat Robertson has lost his wife Dede Robertson who died on April 20th, 2022 at the age of 94 in her home in Virginia Beach.

Though Dede Robertson is gone, we can’t take anything away from Pat Robertson who has spent so many years building his family. Pat and Dede married in 1954 and lived together ever since. Their children are Gordon P. Robertson, Elizabeth Faith Robertson, Tim Robertson, and Anne Carter Robertson.

We explore further in this article to know more about Anne Carter Robertson.

Anne Carter Robertson has not followed her father to go into politics or televangelism but she is only known to be a musician.
Just like her sister Elizabeth Faith Robertson, Anne Carter Robertson has not disclosed her age, place of birth, early childhood life, education background, and spouse to the media and the general public.

In fact, she is shying away from the media.
