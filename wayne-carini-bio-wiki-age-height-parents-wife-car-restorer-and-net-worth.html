Wayne Carini Biography

Wayne Carini is an American car restorer and TV personality who was born on October 13, 1951, in Portland, Connecticut, United States. He stars in the Chasing Classic Cars TV series.

Wayne is the son of Robert (Bob) Carini who owned a collision repair as well as auto restoration business in Glastonbury, Connecticut, Wayne eventually inherited half of the family business as a gift.

    Wayne Carini Career

    Wayne began his career in his father’s shop while still in grade school, working together on classics including Duesenbergs, Lincolns, Packards and Ford Model As. He has served as a grand marshal at the Klingberg Vintage Motorcar Festival, conducted in New Britain, Connecticut. Wayne has been involved in the TV show My Classic Car as himself and he appeared in Overhaulin’, a reality TV series.

    Wayne’s lifelong chasing of vintage cars was noticed by Jim Astrausky, the chief of Essex Television Group Inc.. Astrausky approached Wayne for a television show. The show airs on the Motor Trend channel.
