Cardi B Pulls Off 3 Skintight Bodysuits in Under 24 Hours in Paris

Cardi B’s whirlwind fashion tour in Paris reached a fever pitch earlier this week. The Grammy winner displayed her jaw-dropping style with three consecutive body-hugging looks—all delivered within a span of just 24 hours.

First up, Cardi went shopping at Hermès in a beige catsuit from Valentino’s Spring/Summer 2023 collection, which was emblazoned with the Italian house’s black logo pattern all over. The suit came with a matching cape with a long, dramatic train. She paired the standout look with Valentino’s Garavani Locò bag, strappy black heels, and glittering jewel-encrusted chandelier earrings.

Getty Images

Getty Images

The next day, the rapper was photographed exiting her hotel while dressed in yet another skintight catsuit. That time, the piece featured graphic illustrations in black, including drawings of the sun, anatomical figures, body parts, and chains. She upped the ante with her accessories, wearing stacks of chunky bracelets, choker necklaces, and hoop earrings in a distressed silver hue. She capped off the look with metallic silver heels with matching silver balls on the strap.

Getty Images

Getty Images

Later in the day, Cardi did a quick outfit change to sit front row at Balenciaga’s runway show, where she was accompanied by husband Offset. The occasion called for a clingy bodysuit with a plunging neckline and shimmery black sequins. Attached at the piece’s hip was a large floor-length taffeta bow, also in black. She completed the look with matching pointed heeled booties and the same pair of chandelier earrings she had worn the day before.

Meanwhile, rapper Offset coordinated in a hooded black jacket, baggy black pants, and thick platform combat boots.
