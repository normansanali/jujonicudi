Sister Wives' Mykelti Brown and Husband Tony's Family Album

Family of five! Sister Wives’ star Mykelti Brown and husband Antonio “Tony” Padron are the proud parents of daughter Avalon and twin boys Archer and Ace.

The TLC personality — who is one of the six children that Kody Brown shares with ex Christine Brown — tied the knot with the banking professional in December 2016 after dating for a little more than a year. In September 2020, the couple announced they were expecting their first child.

“Tony & I are PREGNANT. We’ve been planning this since we were married almost four years ago,” Mykelti wrote via Instagram at the time. “So happy that it’s finally happened. We are expecting the baby in March 2021. Happy beyond belief and I can’t wait to share another amazing experience with my @tonychessnut husband.”

Mykelti gave birth to Avalon in April 2021. “Basically the cutest ever 🤗😍 only extremely obsessed with my Avalon Asa gorgeous and full of life,” she wrote alongside the first solo snap of her newborn. Since welcoming Avalon, Mykelti has documented her little one’s growth on her respective social media.

“‘Reading is the gateway skill that makes all other learning possible’ I think Miss Avalon will be smart and fun,” she captioned a snap of her daughter reading. “Her personality is changing and developing every day and I’m loving every second of it.”

In June 2022, the Utah natives revealed they were expanding their family once again.

“WE’RE HAVING TWINS !!!! Miss Avalon will be a big sister in December (twins come earlier so probably before then),” the reality star wrote via Instagram at the time. “Tony and I definitely weren’t planning this or expecting it but we’re both excited for our upcoming adventure and all the chaos and love it brings.”

The parents welcomed their twin boys, Archer and Ace, in November 2022. The mother of three told TLC that she was “so happy” her boys were there and that they were “all doing well.”

Padron, for his part, gushed about his growing family, adding, “I’m glad my sons have finally come to play!”

After settling in with her newest kiddos, Mykelti shared how Avalon was handling her big sister duties.
