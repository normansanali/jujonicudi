Cordell Broadus Bio, Age, Facts, Family Life of Snoop Doggs Son

Cordell Broadus (born February 21, 1997) is an American Former Football Player, Model, Filmmaker, Social Media Personality, and Celebrity Kid from Los Angeles, California. He is well acknowledged as the younger boy of Snoop Dogg (rapper, singer, producer, entrepreneur, and actor).

When it comes to the American music industry, Snoop is one of the biggest stars. He made debut with his album titled “Doggystyle” in 1993. In fact, his work received quadruple platinum certification in 1994.

Some of his other popular works are “Tha Doggfather”, “Tha Last Meal”, “Tha Blue Carpet Treatment”, “Malice ‘n Wonderland”, “Ego Trippin”, and much more. Cordell started his career as a footballer during his school days.

Furthermore, he received offers from elite universities such as Florida State, LSU, Duke, Notre Dame, and Washington State. Finally, Broadus chose UCLA and played for the college team for many years.

He used to perform as a wide receiver and became a star player. However, his passion changed after some time and he quit football in 2016.

Snoop Dogg’s son stepped into this world and came to his parents in the year 1997 and was born on the 21st of February of that year. Cordell Broadus’ age is 22 years old, as of 2019. As per Wikipedia, he also has a step-brother “Julian Corrie Broadus” from his dad’s side. Julian was born to Calvin Cordozar’s mistress “Laurie Holmond”. The young former player completed his school education from Bishop Gorman High School.

His UCLA recruitment also covered by ESPN and became a five-part miniseries. He received a scholarship for UCLA’s football team. After quitting football, he continued college and graduated with a bachelors’ degree.
