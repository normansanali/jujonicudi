How do you adjust a French door on a Whirlpool refrigerator?

Answer

Open-ended or adjustable wrenches of 5/16-inch diameter are used to raise the door and lower it. The alignment screw should be turned counterclockwise to raise and counterclockwise to lower the door. Continue to make slight adjustments until both doors are centred in their apertures and the tops of the doors are level with one another.
