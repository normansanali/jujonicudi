Tina Louise Net Worth

Tina Louise is an American actress best known for her role as Ginger Grant on the classic television series Gilligan's Island. Born in New York City on February 11, 1934, Louise's career began in the 1950s with roles in films such as God's Little Acre, for which she won a Golden Globe Award for New Star of the Year. She has since appeared in numerous films and television shows, and her role as Ginger on Gilligan's Island remains one of her most iconic.
