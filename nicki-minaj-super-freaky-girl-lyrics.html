Super Freaky Girl Lyrics By Nicki Minaj The Current Online

Nicki Minaj’s “Super Freaky Girl” shot to the top of the Billboard Hot 100, giving her her first No. 1 as a solo artist and her third overall.

Since Lauryn Hill’s “Doo Wop (That Thing)” in 1998, Minaj’s “Super Freaky Girl,” which samples Rick James’ 1981 hit “Super Freak,” is the first hip-hop song by a woman to go straight to No. 1. Minaj’s last two No. 1 songs were “Trollz” with 6ix9ine and “Say So” by Doja Cat, both of which came out in 2020. Her new No. 1 song is also from 2020.
